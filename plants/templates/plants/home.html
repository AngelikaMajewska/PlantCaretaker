Manage and track watering of all your plants

Catalog

For users without account, you can use it as a source of information. Once you log in. you can also add plants to the wishlist, remove them from it and see which of the cataloged plants you already have.

Open any plant and see all the details you need to take care of it. You can also download all this information in the PDF format.

See all the data of each owned plant collected in one place.

Each plant from the list of your owned plants has its separate page to track the watering, fertilizing and make notes. No matter how many plants you have, you can easily focus on the needs of each of them without forgetting your observations.

AI features that can save the day

With this feature implemented, you can send the image of your plant to get AI generated diagnosis that can help you find a root cause of the issues your beloved plant experiences.

Use of AI doesn't end here. Give it a picture of a plant you don't know the name of and let AI give its own guesses what that might me.

Calendar

See all your planned events and keep finished ones just below. Check without a hassle what already happened and do not worry about repeating some task.

Add any event to the calendar and if you want - link it to the plant.
